## What Calc is

Calc is the spreadsheet package. Workbooks are stored as `.xlsx` files in [Drive](help://drive:getting-started) — the file *is* the spreadsheet, and Drive's sharing controls govern who can open it. Two people opening the same workbook see each other's edits in real time.

## Opening a workbook

Three ways to open a spreadsheet:

- From the nav rail, click **Calc** to see every workbook in this org, then click one.
- From **Drive**, click any `.xlsx` file. The preview opens with an **Open in Calc** action.
- From a share link someone sent you — if the link grants access, the workbook opens directly.

## Creating a new workbook

From the Calc index, click **New spreadsheet**. A blank workbook is created in Drive and opens in the editor. You can also use **File → New spreadsheet** from inside any open workbook.

## The editor at a glance

- **Menu bar** — File, Edit, View, Format, Data, Help. Most actions live here.
- **Toolbar** — formatting controls (bold, fill, borders, number format), plus **Insert pivot table**.
- **Formula bar** — shows the active cell's content; type a formula starting with `=`.
- **Grid** — the cells. Click to select, drag to extend, double-click (or just start typing) to edit.
- **Sheet tabs** — at the bottom, one per sheet. Right-click a tab for rename / color / hide / delete.

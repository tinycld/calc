## Picking up where you left off

Click the **Calc** icon in the left rail and Calc reopens the last spreadsheet you had open — your work is one click away from anywhere in the app. The rail remembers per package, so the **Text** icon does the same for documents.

If you've never opened a spreadsheet in this workspace, the Calc icon brings you to a small landing panel with three options instead.

## The landing panel

You'll see the panel on a fresh workspace, after the device's storage has been cleared, or when you sign in on a new browser. It offers three ways to start:

- **New sheet** — creates a blank spreadsheet and drops you straight into the editor.
- **Upload files** — picks one or more `.xlsx` or `.csv` files from your device. A single file opens directly; multiple files land in [Drive's recent view](help://drive:getting-started) so you can choose which to open first. CSVs are previewed before import so you can check the delimiter.
- **Recent files** — sends you to Drive's **Recent** view, where every package's recently-touched files are listed together.

## Resetting the rail

There's no manual "forget" — the rail simply updates whenever you open a different spreadsheet. Closing the tab or signing out doesn't reset it; opening a new file does.
